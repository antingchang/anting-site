Lost Communications 失絡之聲 transforms field recordings of birds from the UK, Mongolia, Sichuan, Hong Kong, and Taiwan into dark, experimental electronic soundscapes. Created by An-Ting in collaboration with creative technologist Ian Gallagher, the album draws from extraordinary journeys — high-altitude explorations to ayahuasca rituals — acting as a bridge between human creativity and nature’s advanced systems.
